01 RANK_FINAL — The Core Metric Primary Output

Full composite ranking. If only one column could be used from the entire system — this is the one. Integrates: RS vs market, benchmark outperformance, price structure, behavioral consistency, and movement quality.

RangeInterpretationProfile
1 – 10Top-tier leadershipStrong RS, near highs, excess return positive
11 – 30Strong candidatesSolid structure, good relative performance
31 – 60Moderate rankingMixed signals, some strength but inconsistencies
60+Weak positioningLagging, poor structure, or high drawdown
02 Asset Classification — advanced_type Behavioral Profile
TypeDescriptionAction
alpha Cleanest leadership. Strong RS + structural consistency. Sustainable. Full priority
alpha_fast Aggressive momentum. High speed. Early/accelerating phases. Active monitoring
early_trend Emerging leadership. Early signs before move is confirmed. Position early
rebound Mean reversion from drawdown. Technical — not structural. Caution
neutral No clear dominant signal. Lacks evidence of leadership. Deprioritize
Golden Rule: Always filter advanced_type first. A rebound asset with an A+ Breakout grade is a technical recovery — not an alpha opportunity.
Best combo: alpha or alpha_fast + RANK_FINAL < 15 + A+ Breakout = maximum conviction.
Avoid: rebound type regardless of Breakout grade unless Historical shows transition to alpha.
03 Breakout Grades — Setup Quality Tactical Signal
GradeRRConvictionType RequiredAction
A+ > 2.5 High alpha / alpha_fast Immediate priority
A > 1.8 Medium–High alpha / early_trend High priority — act with conviction
B > 1.2 Medium Any alpha type Monitor — act on confirmation
C < 1.2 Low–Medium Any Awareness only — do not prioritize

RR = Total Return ÷ Max Drawdown — movement quality score. Breakouts measure setup quality. RANK_FINAL measures structural leadership. Maximum conviction requires both.

06 Commodity Cycle Phases Market Structure
PHASE 1
Early Accumulation
Gold begins leading. 1–3 large miners enter Top 20. Most miners flat or declining.
Signal: Gold benchmark positive excess. A+ setups isolated in gold/large miners.
PHASE 2
Expansion
Silver accelerates. Mid-tier miners appear in Leaders. Breakouts active across segments.
Signal: Leaders broadening. RS_score rising broadly. Multiple A/A+ setups.
PHASE 3
Broad Participation
Juniors explode. Maximum breadth. A+ setups become scarce. Retail participants enter.
Signal: Juniors in Leaders. Breakouts crowded. Hard to find clean A+ setups.
PHASE 4
Correction
Juniors fall first. Mid-tiers follow. Gold/silver hold longer. Breakouts go quiet.
Signal: Leaders contracting. Mass exits in Historical. Breakouts drying up.
